Premier League Soccer Countdown Timer

Project date: January 2015 - Present

I'm an avid soccer fan and follow the English Premier League quite religiously. So I decided to create a countdown timer which tells me when the next Chelsea game is. So I set about constructing this timer which retrieves data wirelessly from the internet and displays a countdown in days, hours, minutes and seconds onto a 16x2 LCD.

Two player checkers in Python

Project date: September - December 2014

I learned a tremendous amount of Python in the past four months which I put to use by creating this two player checkers game. I used Tkinter once again to build this bad boy. The checkerboard and pieces are just shapes that I defined in Tkinter. The pieces are circles that have the drag option enabled. The challenging part was defining the logic behind every move that was made by this so-called "dragging" of pieces.

Turn it Off!!! Power Management System

Project date: May 2013 - March 2014

This power management system was designed to reduce power consumption due to idle devices such as microwaves and device chargers in the typical residential home. The Turn It Off!!! Power Management System reduced power consumption of such devices by controlling power to each circuit at the source. This embedded system, located at the service entrance to the residential home, provides complete control over individual circuits via internet scheduling done through Google Calendar and relays installed after the branch circuit breakers.
